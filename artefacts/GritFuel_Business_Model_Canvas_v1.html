Cost Structure

Variable (per client)

  • Partner delivery — 75% of credit pool (invisible to client)
  • Paid media — pass-through at cost, no markup
  • Credit-layer margin: 25%

Fixed / service layer

  • Strategists (binding constraint — 6 clients / FTE)
  • Internal delivery: content · SEO · PR · social · email
  • Operations · Client Success · tooling
  • Fixed-layer margin: 47%

€300k pre-seed allocation

  • 40% Go-to-market (conferences · paid · visits)
  • 27% Tech & product (dev + Tech Lead)
  • 25% Operating runway buffer (ramp gap)
  • 8% Setup & working capital (BV · legal · tools)

Scaling dynamics

  • All-in GM: 35% · GM/client/mo: €1,697

Open questions for iteration

Things this v1 doesn't resolve — worth flagging before v2

Commercial

  • Should the "Month 0" diagnostic replace the discovery call entirely, and be mandatory before any subscription?
  • Does a flat-fee Core tier cannibalise the credit model, or recover otherwise-lost segment?
  • What triggers credit-pool resizing across tiers? Should Performance be uncapped at €X/credit?

Structural

  • Should VCs/accelerators sit in Key Partners (referral channel) or Customer Segments (paying partner)?
  • Does SSA need its own canvas, or is it a variation of this one with pricing + partner differences?
  • Do we optimise Y1–Y2 for client count (moat) or ARPU (margin)?

Operational

  • What's the SLA on the Compound Score being delivered every month — and what happens when it slips?
  • How does credit rollover handle churn — refund, lapse, or credit-back?
  • When does the senior-strategist pool become a formal talent program vs. ad-hoc contracting?
