03 · How reviews work

Async-first. One live anchor per month.

GritFuel operates on an async-first review model. This keeps delivery moving, protects your calendar, and means you never need to book a call to unblock work. Every deliverable ships with a structured review brief and a 72-hour feedback window.

How each deliverable is reviewed

  • Delivered to the shared workspace — Google Drive or Notion. You receive a notification with a direct link.
  • Review brief included — each deliverable ships with a short note: what to focus on, what decision to make, what feedback format works best.
  • Feedback via inline comments — one consolidated round per deliverable keeps things clean.
  • 72-hour window — standard review period. If feedback isn't received, we follow up once and the revision carries forward.

Live sessions

  • Monthly Playbook review (30 min) — included in all tiers. Walk through the Dashboard, confirm this month's priorities, flag any strategic shifts.
  • Strategy sessions (à la carte) — when scope genuinely needs a live working session (GTM workshop, positioning sprint). Priced in credits against the ALC Menu.

Revision rounds

One revision round per deliverable is already priced into the initiative. Additional rounds draw from your monthly allocation or rollover — you'll always know the credit cost before we proceed.
