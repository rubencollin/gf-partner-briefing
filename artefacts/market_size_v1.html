Key assumptions

Methodology notes — challenge these

EU digital-first filter (20%): Applied conservatively. Post-COVID digital acceleration suggests this could be 25–30%, which would expand the TAM by ~60,000 companies.
SSA ACV (EUR 2,500/mo): Blended rate. VC-backed companies in Lagos and Nairobi benchmarking against USD costs may sustain full EUR 5,000/mo — would increase SSA SAM revenue by ~2×.
EU SAM filter (40% of TAM): Based on proportion at active growth inflection point. If paid media intent is broader, this could be 50%+ — SAM expands to ~62,500 companies.
EU → Africa segment: Smaller absolute number but high-margin. Full EU pricing applies. Grows as African markets attract more European commercial interest post-2025.
SOM scaling: Assumes linear headcount growth. GritFuel OS automation (80% target) could significantly improve clients-per-strategist ratio, accelerating Year 3 capacity.
ACV blend: EUR 5,000/mo EU average assumes ~50% Core / 35% Growth / 15% Performance mix. If Performance tier adoption is higher, ACV and margin improve materially.
