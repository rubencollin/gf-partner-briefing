Escalation & Handoff Logic

Partner Routing · Trigger Conditions · Handoff Templates. Companion spec behind Tool 04 — Escalation Router.

01Overview & Purpose

This document defines the escalation and partner routing logic for GritFuel. It specifies the conditions under which a client brief moves beyond GritFuel's core delivery team, which partner leads, and how the handoff is managed — both internally and in client communications.

Three principles govern all routing decisions:

1. GritFuel is always the client's single point of contact, regardless of who delivers.
2. Partner names (.frank, Bluegyfu, BFL) are never referenced in client communications.
3. Escalation does not mean loss of control — GritFuel orchestrates, partners deliver.

07Process Steps

When a brief arrives requiring escalation:

  1. Strategist identifies triggers (service type, complexity flags, client signals, compounding trigger).
  2. Run brief through the routing tool to determine primary partner and escalation level.
  3. Complete the internal handoff brief template.
  4. Brief the partner lead before responding to the client.
  5. Send the client-facing message within 2 business days of the initial ask.
  6. Update the monthly playbook to reflect the impact on credit allocation and timeline.
  7. After delivery, assess whether the trigger is recurring — if so, update the client's onboarding profile.
